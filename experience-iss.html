Credits

Dream2050 is a concept and demonstration project created for the NASA Space Apps Challenge 2025. The demo highlights station systems, science operations, and crew workflows—compiled from public-domain resources, open-source tooling, and team contributions.

Project credits

  • Imagery & Data: NASA imagery, datasets, and public-domain resources used under their respective terms.
  • Engine & Tools: Unreal Engine 5 and standard development tooling used for the simulated experience.
  • Design & UI: Open-source icons and web fonts used for the site interface and UI mockups.
  • Team: Team Dream2050 — concept, research, 3D/Unity/Unreal development, content, and testing.
