About Dream2050

Dream2050 is our NASA Space Apps Challenge 2025 project envisioning the International Space Station as a living, learning laboratory in the mid-21st century. We combine systems thinking, space operations knowledge, and humanâ€“AI teaming to simulate daily station life.

  • Station Systems: Track power, thermal control, life support, and comms with simplified dashboards.
  • Science Ops: Schedule payloads, monitor experiment health, and react to anomalies.
  • Crew Workflow: Balance exercise, EVA prep, maintenance, research, and downtime.
  • AI Astrobot: An assistant designed to co-plan tasks, explain telemetry, and simulate crew dialogues.
